## Common Questions

### "Where is Lead Scoring?"
Go to `/leads` page ‚Üí Click the "Scoring" tab at the top ‚Üí Create scoring rules

### "Where is Workflow Analytics?"
Go to `/automations` ‚Üí Select a workflow from the list ‚Üí Click the "Analytics" tab

### "Where are Webhooks?"
Open Settings (‚åò. or click your profile) ‚Üí Click "Integrations" ‚Üí Scroll to "Webhooks" section

### "How do I test A/B variants?"
Go to `/forms` ‚Üí Select a form ‚Üí Click "A/B Tests" tab ‚Üí Create variant ‚Üí Set traffic % ‚Üí View analytics

### "Where is the Multi-Channel Inbox?"
Go to `/inbox` page (Premium feature only) - shows SMS, Email, Voice in unified interface

### "How do I access Agent Memory?"
Go to `/ai-agents` ‚Üí Click on an agent card ‚Üí Click the "Memory" tab
